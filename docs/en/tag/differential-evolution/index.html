Overview of Differential Evolution

Introduction to DE algorithm Differential Evolution (DE) was proposed by Storn et al. in 1995. Like other evolutionary algorithms, DE is a stochastic model that simulates biological evolution. Through repeated iterations, individuals that adapt to the environment are preserved. However, compared with evolutionary algorithms, DE retains the global search strategy based on the population, and uses real number encoding, simple mutation operations based on differences, and one-to-one competition survival strategies to reduce the complexity of genetic operations...
