Cybercrime Overview

one. The concept of cybercrime refers to the general term for actors who use computer technology to attack their systems or information with the help of the network, destroy or use the network to commit other crimes. It includes crimes committed by perpetrators using their programming, encryption, and decoding technologies or tools on the Internet. It also includes crimes committed by perpetrators who use software instructions, network system or product encryption and other technical and legal loopholes to commit crimes interactively inside and outside the network.
