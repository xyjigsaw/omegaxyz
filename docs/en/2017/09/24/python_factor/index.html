Python factoring

Programming LanguagesTechnologyPythonPython
The process of decomposing a composite number into the product of several prime factors, that is, finding the prime factors, is called decomposing prime factors.
Factoring prime factors only applies to composite numbers. (Decomposing prime factors is also called decomposing prime factors) To find the prime factors of a number, start from the smallest prime number and divide until the result is a prime number. The formula for decomposing prime factors is called short division., has similar properties to division, and can also be used to find common factors of multiple numbers.
The following method uses the method of generating random numbers to decompose factors:
