Multi-objective CSO algorithm (MOCSO) understanding

TechnologyMachine LearningEvolutionary Algorithms多目标优化算法PSO算法
MOCSO（Multi-Objective Competitive Swarm Optimizer) is a variant of PSO (Particle Swarm Optimization algorithm) Competitive particle swarm optimization algorithm (MOCSO) based on multi-objective optimization Summary: In evolutionary computation, exploring effective multi-objective optimization techniques is worthy of attention. We propose a multi-objective optimization algorithm based on the recently developed swarm intelligence paradigm. On the basis of this algorithm, we propose a guided learning strategy for learning update fitting to accelerate the convergence speed and avoid the reduction of diversity of multi-objective optimization algorithms. The chosen method randomly selects two solutions within the existing population to guide each particle's flight. The particles in most particle swarm optimization algorithms are usually judged based on previous information (P best and G best). The performance of this algorithm is verified through comparison with several state-of-the-art multi-objective evolutionary algorithms, including three existing multi-objective particle swarm optimization algorithms and three popular multi-objective algorithms based on genetic algorithms. Experimental results show that this algorithm has the superiority of multi-objective optimization. Keywords: multi-objective optimization, competitive swarm algorithm, multi-objective evolutionary algorithm, swarm intelligence algorithm. Competitive Particle Optimization Algorithm The main difference from PSO: the pairwise competition mechanism (two solutions randomly selected from elite particles in the existing group) is used to guide the search instead of pbest &gbest Main contributions: 1. A competition mechanism strategy based on the leader particle to update particles. Two solutions randomly selected from elite particles in the existing population can better balance convergence and diversity. 2.
