A brief analysis of the characteristics and performance of computer science in economic crimes

IdeasDeveloper LifeCyber Crime

summary:

The era of big data has arrived, and at the same time, computer economic crime is also becoming more and more serious. It not only poses a threat to the national security and social culture of various countries, but also disrupts the economic order of society and causes huge economic losses to ordinary third parties, such as using the Internet for fund-raising fraud, pyramid schemes, money laundering, dissemination and production of pirated and pornographic products, etc. In computer economic crimes, the downloading and dissemination of network software are important features of modern computer economic crimes. This article will use the relevant knowledge of economic criminal law courses to briefly analyze the characteristics and performance of computer science in economic crimes. .Keywords: economic crime, computer, software, Internet

1. The relationship between computer science and economic criminal law

Computer science definition: The science that studies computers and various phenomena and laws around them, that is, the discipline that studies the structure of computer systems, program systems (i.e. software), artificial intelligence, and the nature and problems of computing itself. Economic criminal law: Refers to all criminal law norms related to economic activities and economic interests. Broadly speaking, it involves the largest range of economic crimes, as well as all legal norms with a criminal nature in economic administrative regulations. The relationship between the two: There was no necessary connection between the two before the development of specific network technology. After the improvement of computer technology, crimes such as illegal intrusion into computer information systems and crimes of damaging computer information systems gradually appeared in our country. With the popularization of smartphones and the rapid growth of the number of people accessing the Internet, my country's network security has gradually improved (but is not perfect). The human, material, financial and time costs of intruding computers with certain technical skills have increased accordingly. People with criminal intentions are more likely to make relatively easy network software to steal users' personal property or private information (Ezubao case); a small number of computer professionals who do not have criminal intentions often cross the bottom line of the law while providing technology (Quaibo case).

2. Performance of computer science in economic crimes

1. Illegal application of the Internet in economic crimes

Cyber ​​economic crime refers to the general term for actors who use computer technology to attack their systems or information with the help of the Internet, destroy or use the Internet to commit economic crimes. This includes economic crimes committed interactively inside and outside the network by using loopholes in software instructions, network systems or product encryption, and other technical and legal regulations. It also includes economic crimes committed by actors in network systems by virtue of their specific position as a network service provider or other methods. In short, cyber economic crimes are crimes committed against and using the Internet. The essential characteristic of cyber economic crimes is to endanger the market economic order. There are generally 8 types of crimes committed using computer networks: 1 Using computers to commit financial fraud 2 Using computers to commit theft 3 Using computers to commit corruption and misappropriation of public funds 4 The crime of using computers to steal state secrets 5 Electronic blackmail 6 Online smuggling and illegal online transactions 7 Illegally absorbing deposits from the public 8 Crimes of intellectual property infringement Let’s first look at financial cybercrime in Russia. Kaspersky antivirus software company issued a report in 2016. The report assessed the harm caused to enterprises by Russian-speaking cybercriminals arrested in the past few years. It also introduced the products and services that exist in the Russian underground market, revealing the structure of a typical Russian cybercriminal organization and the functions of each member. The report shows that between 2012 and 2015, law enforcement agencies in different countries around the world arrested more than 160 suspects from Russia and neighboring countries, all of whom are suspected of committing financial cybercrime on a global scale. It is estimated that the losses caused by these cybercriminals' crimes exceed $790 million. We can see that this financial cybercriminal organization recruits computer professionals and economic management professionals to steal national or personal property through network technology. (Figure 1: How financial cybercrimes are structured and operated)Let's analyze this diagram. The fund flow manager transfers the funds obtained from the attacked financial account to the account provided by the money mule manager. The money mule manager then directs his subordinates to transfer the funds to other places. Some of the stolen funds fall into the hands of the service leader, and the remaining funds are transferred to the leaders of the cybercrime organization.

2.Criminal forms and characteristics of computer software (taking P2P software as an example)

P2P explained: Peer-to-peer network, that is, peer-to-peer computer network, is a distributed application architecture that distributes tasks and workloads among peers. It is a networking or network form formed by the peer-to-peer computing model at the application layer. "Peer" means "peer, partner, counterpart" in English. Therefore, literally, P2P can be understood as peer-to-peer computing or peer-to-peer network. Some domestic media translate P2P as "point-to-point" or "end-to-end", and academic circles collectively call it peer-to-peer networking or peer-to-peer computing. It can be defined as: network participants share part of the hardware resources they own (processing power, storage capacity, network connection capabilities, printers, etc.). These shared resources provide services and content through the network and can be directly accessed by other peer nodes (Peer) without going through intermediate entities. Participants in this network are both providers of resources, services and content (Server), and acquirers of resources, services and content (Client). Characteristics of crime: ①Diversified and intelligent criminal methodsThe rapid development of information networks and the popularization and promotion of information technology have provided Internet financial criminals with a third-party payment platform.②There are many victims and the case covers a wide range of areasDue to the connectivity of the Internet, crime victims appear in different industries, people, and regions.③The social harm is hugeThe higher the popularity of the Internet, the greater the harm of cyber crimes, and the harm of cyber economic crimes is far beyond that of ordinary traditional crimes. Compared with economic cyber crimes involving property, they can easily cause losses of millions, tens of millions or even hundreds of millions of yuan.④Easily trigger mass incidentsDue to the current low threshold for entry into the P2P online lending industry, low capital requirements, and uneven risk control capabilities of platform entrepreneurs, the P2P online lending industry is a mixed bag. Once the borrower defaults or there are problems with the operation of the online platform, it is difficult to guarantee the rights and interests of the borrower.Personal understanding of Kuaibo CompanyThe P2P software of Kuaibo Company is an emerging model for realizing video search through network storage space. While it brings convenience to people, it also accumulates many risks and becomes an accomplice in crimes such as the spread of obscene materials. Based on the benefits brought by network development and the modesty of criminal law, criminal law should be extremely cautious when intervening in the governance of P2P software. On the one hand, we cannot just sit idly by and ignore the illegal and criminal activities in software because it brings us huge economic benefits. On the other hand, we should not over-regulate the operating market and hinder the development of the entire computer field because of the shortcomings of P2P software. When governing online video playback platforms, it is necessary to strictly examine the role of neutral behavior in promoting crime, the substitutability of neutral behavior, the closeness with daily life, and the determination of the review limits of neutral behavior to draw the line between normal operating behavior and crime, so as to achieve the unity of legal effects and social effects, and to achieve the unity of formal rationality and substantive rationality.

3. Prevention of computer economic crimes and protection of network information security

1.General measures:

① Carry out online moral education ② Strengthen network security management ③Standardize network operations in accordance with the law and improve the network legal system ④Severely crack down on computer network crimes

2.Computer (software and network) prevention measures against cybercrimes:

For software companies that serve the government, they can recruit talents to develop technology to combat cyber economic crimes.
