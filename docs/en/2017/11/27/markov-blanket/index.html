Markov Blankets

TechnologyMachine Learning离散数学朴素贝叶斯
When it comes to Markov blankets, there will be a bunch of concepts that are very similar in name, such as Markov Chain (MC), Hidden Markov Model (HMM), Markov Random Field (MarkovRandom Field, MRF), etc. In fact, the Markov blanket is different from these concepts in that it is a local concept rather than an overall model-level concept. The following content mainly refers to [He Xian. Research on Markov blanket discovery algorithm based on Bayesian network [D]. University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, 2012.].
