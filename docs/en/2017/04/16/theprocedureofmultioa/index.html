The general process of multi-objective evolutionary algorithms

技术域机器学习进化算法多目标优化算法
① Randomly generate population P. ②P uses EA (evolutionary algorithm) to obtain G. ③Construct the non-dominated solution set NDset (non dominated set) of P∪G. (This involves how to construct a non-dominated solution set) ④Adjust the size of NDset and make it meet the distribution requirements. (This involves what strategies to use to adjust the size of NDset and how to maintain the diversity and distribution of non-dominated sets) ⑤If P<=NDset, loop to step ②, otherwise go to ⑥. ⑥End when the conditions are met.
