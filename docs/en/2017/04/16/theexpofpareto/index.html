Some explanations of Pareto optimal solutions

TechnologyMachine LearningEvolutionary Algorithms多目标优化算法
1. Pareto optimal solution is also called non-dominated solution and non-dominant solution. Pareto optimal solution set is also called non-inferior solution, non-dominated solution set and non-dominant solution set. 2. The figure below is a schematic diagram of the distribution of the final pareto front (pareto front) of the minimized two-objective optimization problem. The smaller the f1 and f2 objective values are, the better. The solid and dotted lines are the feasible region. The solid line represents the pareto front, that is, the surface composed of the target vectors corresponding to all pareto optimal solutions. A multi-objective optimization problem corresponds to a pareto front.3. The three points A, B, and C are located on the pareto frontier. The solutions of these three points are the pareto optimal solutions. There is no dominance or dominance relationship between them. The solutions to points D, E, and F are feasible solutions and are not pareto optimal solutions. 4. The solution at point A dominates the solution at point F, or compared to the solution at point F, the solution at point A is pareto dominant. Similarly, there is a dominance relationship or dominance relationship between points B and C and points D, E, and F.
