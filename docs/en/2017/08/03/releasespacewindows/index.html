What should I do if the Windows system C drive is getting bigger and bigger (including win10)

TechnologyWindows

Using 360 and CCleaner can no longer meet the needs of users. In addition, Win10 will generate many temporary files during the update process, and the windows folder on the C drive will become larger and larger.
