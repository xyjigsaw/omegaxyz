When the rules are absolutely fair, to whom does social wealth flow?

Programming LanguagesC & C++TechnologyIdeas随笔Developer LifeC++CIdeas
There is a very interesting question from Zhihu: There are 100 people in the room, each of them has 100 yuan. If every minute, everyone takes out one yuan and randomly gives it to another person, what is the final wealth distribution of these 100 people?This problem can be seen as a small experiment, simulating the flow of social wealth when the rules are absolutely fair. If wealth flows randomly, will the gap between rich and poor disappear? The Urban Data Group simulated this process using a program: In this way, 80% of the money goes into the pockets of 20%.Even under the fairest rules, the world still shows a cruel side.What's more, real wealth flows are not random. There is a joke that goes like this: Let me tell you a way to make two million in a year... deposit 100 million in the bank regularly and make two million in a year. A joke is a joke, and it is true that original capital is often one of the most effective ways to accumulate wealth. For experimental results on allowing debt, wealth accumulation of the rich second generation, and subsistence allowance + taxation, please visit the original author:http://www.sohu.com/a/159059809_167388Of course, some experts on Zhihu raised objections to the author of the original article: This article made a lot of classic mistakes in probability theory. The normal distribution he calculated was the money in the hands of each individual. The original article calculated the wealth distribution after group sorting. It was not the same thing at all. . . Please visit:https://www.zhihu.com/pin/877873289857478656I wrote a code myself (no debt allowed):
#include<iostream>
#include<ctime>
#include<stdlib.h>
#include<algorithm>
#include<cstdio>

using namespace std;

int main()
{
    int population=0,total=0,individual=0,generation=0,figure=0,temp=0;
    cout<<"人数:";
    cin>>population;
    cout<<"初始每人拥有的资产:";
    cin>>individual;
    cout<<"迭代次数:";
    cin>>generation;
    cout<<"每一代交换金额:";
    cin>>figure;
    total=population*individual;
    cout<<"社会财富总量:"<<total<<endl;
    cout<<"start"<<endl;
    srand((unsigned)time(NULL));
    int *num=new int[population];
    for(int i=0;i<population;i++)num[i]=individual;
    while(generation>0){
        for(int i=0;i<population;i++){
            if(num[i]>0){
                num[i]-=figure;
                temp=rand()%population;
                num[temp]+=figure;
            }
        }
        generation--;
    }
    total=0;
    for(int i=0;i<population;i++)total+=num[i];
    cout<<"社会财富总量:"<<total<<endl;
    cout<<"每个人的资产"<<endl;
    sort(num,num+population);
    for(int i=0;i<population;i++){
        if((i)%10==0)cout<<endl;
        printf("%5d",num[i]);
    }
    return 0;
}
