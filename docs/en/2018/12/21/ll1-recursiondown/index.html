Recursive descent to implement LL(1) grammar analysis in C language and Python

Programming LanguagesC & C++TechnologyPython编译原理C++Python编译原理
The necessary and sufficient condition for a definite top-down grammatical analysis of sentences of grammar G is that any two productions A—>α|β of G with the same left part satisfy the following conditions:
(1) If neither α nor β can deduce ε, then FIRST(α) ∩ FIRST(β) = ∅.
(2) At most one of α and β can derive ε.
(3) If β *═> ε, then FIRST(α) ∩ FOLLOW(A) = ∅.
A grammar that satisfies the above conditions is called an LL(1) grammar.
example:
E->TE’
E’->+TE’ | ε
T->FT’
T’->*F T’| ε
F->(E) | i

    T();
