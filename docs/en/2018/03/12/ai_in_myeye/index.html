Artificial intelligence in my eyes

Ideas随笔Ideas人工智能
As a computer student, of course it is necessary to talk about artificial intelligence. Speaking of artificial intelligence, the image that pops up in most people’s minds must be a robot. People who know a little bit about it will say professional words such as deep learning, neural network, and machine learning. At this point, those in the know may chuckle secretly. Well, to put it simply, artificial intelligence means different things in different periods. In the past, artificial intelligence may only mean a program that can play backgammon or a chat robot. In the future, artificial intelligence will definitely be a machine with human thinking capabilities, just like the movie AI. It is difficult to predict the future. Back to the present, in today's society, artificial intelligence refers to things such as autonomous driving, AlphaGo, face recognition, speech recognition, and emotion prediction. Yes, have you ever discovered that such artificial intelligence is only used in one aspect and is not a universal intelligence? Of course, the basis of artificial intelligence is machine learning, and the basis of machine learning is mathematics. I won’t delve into what machine learning is here. If you are interested, you can search it on Baidu. Returning to artificial intelligence, the term artificial intelligence appeared in the report of the 19th National Congress of the Communist Party of China. This also reflects that the market attaches great importance to the value of artificial intelligence. From another aspect, it also reflects that artificial intelligence will cause market bubbles. Many companies package themselves as AI-driven companies, but many of them are still technically deficient. For those who try to get into the early stages of AI without understanding the technology, they stand to lose fortunes. And Kai-Fu Lee believes that there will be many economic bubbles in the artificial intelligence market by the end of this year. Similar events have occurred in every corner of the world. For example, in 2000, the bubble created by the rise of the Internet caused many cable TV companies, technology companies, and even Microsoft to fall into trouble. That is a historical event that we are well aware of. Therefore, I think that in such a general environment, the artificial intelligence industry is a mixed bag. If we want to develop real artificial intelligence, we cannot just rely on algorithms such as deep learning. As algorithms develop, hardware and architecture must also keep up. In this way, artificial intelligence will be qualitatively improved.
