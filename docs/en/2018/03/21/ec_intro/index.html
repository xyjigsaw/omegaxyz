Basic methods and ideas of evolutionary computation

TechnologyMachine LearningEvolutionary Algorithms协同进化
In biological systems, evolution is considered a successful adaptive method with good robustness. Basic idea:Darwinian evolution is a robust search and optimization mechanism. Most organisms evolve through natural selection and sexual reproduction. Natural selection determines which individuals in a population survive and reproduce, and sexual reproduction ensures mixing and recombination in the genes of future generations. The principle of natural selection is survival of the fittest and survival of the fittest. Evolutionary computing is a general problem-solving method developed by drawing on natural selection and natural genetic mechanisms in the biological world.Basic method:Evolutionary computing uses simple coding technology to represent various complex structures, and then performs simple genetic operations and natural selection of survival of the fittest to guide learning and determine the search direction. Evolutionary computing uses a population approach to organize searches, allowing it to search multiple areas of the solution space simultaneously, making it particularly suitable for large-scale parallelism. Evolutionary calculation not only achieves high efficiency but also is simple, easy to operate and versatile. At present, evolutionary algorithms have been widely used in many fields such as computer science, engineering technology, management science and social science.
