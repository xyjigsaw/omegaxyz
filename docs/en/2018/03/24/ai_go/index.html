Which company has the best artificial intelligence?

IdeasDeveloper LifeIdeas人工智能
Artificial Intelligence (Artificial Intelligence), the English abbreviation is AI. It is a new technical science that studies and develops theories, methods, technologies and application systems for simulating, extending and expanding human intelligence. Artificial intelligence is a branch of computer science that attempts to understand the essence of intelligence and produce a new intelligent machine that can respond in a manner similar to human intelligence. Research in this field includes robotics, language recognition, image recognition, natural language processing, and expert systems. Since the birth of artificial intelligence, the theory and technology have become increasingly mature, and the application fields have also continued to expand. It can be imagined that the technological products brought by artificial intelligence in the future will be the "containers" of human wisdom. Artificial intelligence can simulate the information process of human consciousness and thinking. Artificial intelligence is not human intelligence, but it can think like humans and may even exceed human intelligence. Artificial intelligence is an extremely challenging science, and people engaged in this work must understand computer knowledge, psychology and philosophy. Artificial intelligence is a very broad science, which consists of different fields, such as machine learning, computer vision, etc. Generally speaking, a main goal of artificial intelligence research is to enable machines to perform complex tasks that usually require human intelligence. But different times and different people have different understandings of this "complex work". In December 2017, artificial intelligence was selected into the "Top Ten Buzzwords in Chinese Media in 2017". AI may be one of the most controversial technologies of this century. But regardless of the controversy, this technological revolution is approaching mankind at an alarming speed. The incubator and birthplace of technology are artificial intelligence research institutions within the world's top universities. Which universities are actively preparing to lead this revolution? Recently, a new study of artificial intelligence graduate programs at top universities across the United States reveals this answer. The following rankings are based on statistical data and questionnaires that reflect the quality of each project, as well as comprehensive rankings after experts and scholars evaluate the projects. According to the report, the top five universities with graduate programs in artificial intelligence are:Carnegie Mellon University MIT Stanford University University of California, Berkeley University of Washington Among all graduate programs at top universities in the United States, "U.S. News & World Report" uses artificial intelligence graduate programs as the main basis for rankings. The strong computer science and engineering departments at Carnegie Mellon University and MIT make them undisputed leaders in the field. Although the University of Washington ranks fifth, its advancement into the top five among many prestigious schools means that Seattle is on the rise and will compete with Silicon Valley in the field of artificial intelligence research. For overall computer science courses, Carnegie Mellon, MIT, Stanford, and UC Berkeley tied for first, while the University of Washington tied with Cornell for sixth.
