How did you become mediocre?

Ideas随笔Ideas
When you wake up and the first thing you do is look for your phone, When you turn on your computer as soon as you get out of bed, When you feel bored and pass the time with games, When you feel lonely and seek excitement on major social networking sites, When you refresh Zhihu, Weibo, and Moments over and over again, When you are addicted to games, novels, and comics and cannot control yourself, When you can't bear the pressure and vent to others, When you feel wronged and can’t help but get angry at others, When you are angry and can’t control yourself and say bad words to others, When you want to give up every time you are treated unfairly, When you encounter difficulties and obstacles and want to retreat, When you are so moved by yourself, When you still think you are different, When you have no plan for your life and career, When you leave campus and still feel that the boat has reached the end of the road, When you are arrogant with your favor, proud with your skills and arrogant, When you follow public opinion and add fuel to the flames, When you accept information and don’t know how to think independently, When you argue just for the sake of arguing, When you deliberately discredit and bring others down, When you can't accept other people's opinions that are different from yours, When you can't accept other people's shortcomings, When you can’t accept your incomplete self, When you think everything is what you think, When you feel that the world will develop as you want, When you feel that man can conquer nature, When you feel like the whole world will make way for you, When you are brainwashed by Chicken Soup for the Soul and no longer be pragmatic, When you are too busy with trivial matters, When you give up your own ideas in order to cater to the leader, When you say something you don’t mean to please, When you want to be like others, When you want others to be like you, When you can’t help yourself for leadership or work, When you feel complacent with a small achievement, When you lose your temper and argue with people over trivial matters, When you are unforgiving and aggressive, When you are extremely dependent on others, When you are making trouble unreasonably, When you refuse to admit your mistakes for the sake of face, When you refuse to let go of your prejudices out of self-esteem, When you blindly believe in authority, When you pursue other people’s glamorous lives, When you realize the illusory value and love, When you abandon your family and your loved ones for your career, When you measure a person with money and work, When you think success means having a lot of money, When you keep looking for reasons for what you do, When you keep making excuses for your mistakes, When you cannot humbly learn from others, When you can’t see others being better than you, When you are jealous of others and complain about others, When you cannot truly bless others, When you talk about other people's rights and wrongs...
