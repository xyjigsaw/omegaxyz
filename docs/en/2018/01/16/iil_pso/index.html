PSO algorithm based on particle interactive learning strategy (IIL-PSO)

TechnologyMachine LearningEvolutionary AlgorithmsPSO算法

IIL-PSO algorithm idea (single objective)

Interswarm Interactive Learning①Initialization: Randomly divide the particles into two groups of equal number, and initialize the position of each group of particles. Velocity, global optimal particle, and individual historical optimal array. ②Iteration and learning: Calculate the probability of each group of particles becoming the learned particle group. The particle updates that become the learned particle swarm are the same as the ordinary PSO algorithm. To become a particle in a learning particle swarm, you not only need to learn the experience of yourself and the particle swarm you are in, but also need to learn the experience of the particle swarm being learned. On this basis, in order to prevent the results of the two particle swarms from being close, the author proposed a velocity mutation strategy and a global optimal vibration strategy.

IILS

（Interswarm Interactive Learning Strategy）① Separate two particle groups and calculate the probability of becoming the particle group being learned.T: Time parameter (the author calls it temperature, the longer the time, the closer the probability of the two particle groups) ② In the learning particle swarm, the learning probability of each particle is calculated. The particles that do not need to learn are updated using a simple PSO algorithm. The particles that need to learn not only need to learn the experience of themselves and the particle swarm they are in, but also need to learn the experience of the particle swarm being learned.Si: particle number N: Total number of particles in the current particle swarm

VMO

（Velocity Mutation Operator）Purpose: In order to prevent the global optimal results of the two particle swarms from being close to each other, the author uses a velocity mutation operator. Operation: Randomly select a particle swarm, randomly select a particle in this particle swarm and the number of dimensions that the particle needs to mutate.

GBVS

（Global Best Vibration Strategy）Purpose: To prevent PSO from falling into local optimality Since Gbest already has a good fitness value, only one dimension needs to be modified here.
