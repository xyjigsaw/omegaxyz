Carry lookahead adder delay analysis

Technology组成原理与体系结构数字逻辑组成原理与体系结构
The carry look ahead adder is a parallel adder designed to improve the ordinary full adder. It is mainly improved to deal with the delay caused by mutual carry when ordinary full adders are connected in series. The carry-lookahead adder does this by adding a logic circuit that is not very complex.
