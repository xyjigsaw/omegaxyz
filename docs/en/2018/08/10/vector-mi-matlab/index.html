Definition of mutual information

Formally, the mutual information of two discrete random variables X and Y can be defined as:where p(x,y) is the joint probability distribution function of X and Y, and p(x) and p(y) are the marginal probability distribution functions of X and Y respectively.In the case of continuous random variables, the summation is replaced by a double definite integral:where p(x,y) is currently the joint probability density function of X and Y, and p(x) and p(y) are the marginal probability density functions of X and Y respectively. The statistical average value of mutual information I(xi;yj) in the joint probability space P(XY). The average mutual information I(X;Y) overcomes the randomness of the mutual information quantity I(xi;yj) and becomes a certain quantity. If the logarithm is based on 2, the unit of mutual information is bit. Intuitively, mutual information measures the information shared by X and Y: it measures how much knowing one of the two variables reduces uncertainty about the other. For example, if X and Y are independent of each other, then knowing that X provides no information about Y and vice versa, their mutual information is zero. At the other extreme, if X is a deterministic function of Y, and Y is also a deterministic function of Therefore, the mutual information in this case is the same as the uncertainty contained in Y (or X) alone, called the entropy of Y (or X). Moreover, this mutual information is the same as the entropy of X and the entropy of Y. (A very special case of this is when X and Y are the same random variables.) Mutual information is the intrinsic dependence between the joint distribution of X and Y relative to the joint distribution if X and Y are assumed to be independent. Mutual information then measures dependence in the following way: I(X; Y) = 0 if and only if X and Y are independent random variables. Main function main.m
clc
u1 = rand(4,1);
u2 = [2;32;6666;5];
wind_size = size(u1,1);
mi = calmi(u1, u2, wind_size);
calmi.m 
%计算两列向量之间的互信息
%u1：输入计算的向量1
%u2：输入计算的向量2
%wind_size：向量的长度
function mi = calmi(u1, u2, wind_size)
x = [u1, u2];
n = wind_size;
[xrow, xcol] = size(x);
bin = zeros(xrow,xcol);
pmf = zeros(n, 2);
for i = 1:2
    minx = min(x(:,i));
    maxx = max(x(:,i));
    binwidth = (maxx - minx) / n;
    edges = minx + binwidth*(0:n);
    histcEdges = [-Inf edges(2:end-1) Inf];
    [occur,bin(:,i)] = histc(x(:,i),histcEdges,1); %通过直方图方式计算单个向量的直方图分布
    pmf(:,i) = occur(1:n)./xrow;
end
%计算u1和u2的联合概率密度
jointOccur = accumarray(bin,1,[n,n]);  %（xi，yi）两个数据同时落入n*n等分方格中的数量即为联合概率密度
jointPmf = jointOccur./xrow;
Hx = -(pmf(:,1))'*log2(pmf(:,1)+eps);
Hy = -(pmf(:,2))'*log2(pmf(:,2)+eps);
Hxy = -(jointPmf(:))'*log2(jointPmf(:)+eps);
MI = Hx+Hy-Hxy;
mi = MI/sqrt(Hx*Hy);
