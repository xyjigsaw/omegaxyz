Introduction to Supervised Word Shift Distance (SWMD)

TechnologyMachine LearningNatural Language ProcessingNatural Language Processing
Disclaimer: This article is part of the final assignment of machine learning

WMD distance

https://omegaxyz.com/2018/11/22/wmd/ we understandWMDdistance, then the question comes,It is good to learn this weight matrix for clustering(Tell us which documents are similar),  but,  used forClassificationBut very bad!Why? Because although some articles have many synonyms, they do not express the same semantics and theme.for example:

I love playing football.

I like playing LOL .

Although it seems that the sentence patterns are similar, they may be classified into the same category, but if the labels are "sports" and "games", it is obvious that they cannot be directly classified using WMD. because,WMDNot joinedfootballand "movement" are strongly related information.

SWMD

In SWMD, the author introduces a supervision mechanism.Introduce an A into the word vector space and define a new movement cost:The linear metric learning algorithm NCA is used to learn A, thus introducing a supervision mechanism.Experimental results: With the addition of a supervision mechanism, the error rate can be reduced to another level. It should be pointed out that the supervision matrix based on metric learning cannot be simply added. On the one hand, the gradient may not exist during the calculation process. On the other hand, KNN-based SWMD will consume a lot of time, so relevant measures need to be taken to solve these problems. Please refer to the original text for details.original: Huang G, Guo C, Kusner M J, et al. Supervised word mover's distance[C]//Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems. 2016: 4862-4870.
