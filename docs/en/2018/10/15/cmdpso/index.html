Multi-objective PSO algorithm based on crowding distance and mutation dominance

TechnologyMachine LearningEvolutionary Algorithms多目标优化算法PSO算法特征选择
This is a paper published by Xue Bing in the first area of ​​cybernetics, which proposes two multi-objective PSO feature selection algorithms, one is NSPSO and the other is CMDPSO. Among them, NSPSO refers to the framework and ideas of NSGA2. Let’s talk about CMDPSO in detail below. The full name of CMDPSO is Crowding, Mutation, Dominance PSO algorithm.

individual crowding distance

Selective sorting is required in the same layer of Fk, sorting according to the individual crowding distance. The individual crowding distance is the distance between individuals i+1 and i-1 adjacent to i on Fk. Its calculation steps are: ① Initialize the distance between individuals in the same layer, let L[i]d=0 (representing the crowding distance of any individual i). ②Arrange the individuals in the same layer in ascending order according to the m-th objective function value. ③ Give selective advantages to individuals on the edge of sorting. ④For the individuals in the middle of the ranking, find the crowding distance:(Where: L[i+1]m is the m-th objective function value fmax of the i+1-th individual, and fmin are the maximum and minimum values of the m-th objective function in the set respectively.) ⑤ For different objective functions, repeat steps ② to ④ to obtain the crowding distance L[i]d of individual i. By limited selection of individuals with larger crowding distances, the calculation results can be evenly distributed in the target space and the diversity of the group can be maintained.
