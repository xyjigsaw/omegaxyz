Problem raising and analysis

Motivation and high-level considerations

The most direct way to improve performance is to increase SIZE, that is, increase depth and width. This is a simple and safe way to train high-quality models, especially on large labeled training sets. However, this simple approach has two major drawbacks.
  • ① As parameters increase, it is easy to lead to overfitting when there is insufficient data.
  • ② The computing resource requirements are high, and many parameters will tend to 0 during the training process, which is a waste of resources.

Solution

solveThe most basic method for the above two problems is to useSparse connections instead of fully connected layers(fully-connected layers), this method is even used during the convolution process.

Theoretical basis

A probability distribution can be represented by a large sparse neural network. The optimal structure is constructed by analyzing the statistical correlation of the activation states of the upper layer and aggregating neurons whose outputs are highly correlated. This is consistent with the Hebbian law in biology: "The responses of certain neurons are basically the same, being excited or inhibited at the same time." Of course, this is also problematic. The basic structure of the computer will be very inefficient when encountering coefficient data calculations. Using sparse matrices will greatly reduce the efficiency.Target:Design one that can take advantage of sparsity while ensuring efficient use of computing resources.

Inception model

Model composition

Inception naminghttps://knowyourmeme.com/memes/we-need-to-go-deeperThis sentence was originally used in a science fiction movie"Inception"appears inThe English name of "Inception" is "Inception”The characters Dom Cobb (played by Leonardo DiCaprio) and Robert Fisher (played by Cillian Murphy) tell the story of implanting a thought in a person's mind.What I said at the beginningWe need to go deeperAlso an expression from the movie Inception, which often uses screenshots from the movie to show image macros and vertical multi-panes.Original Inception version V0:Inception's framework started as a case study, evaluating the hypothetical output of a complex network to approximate the topology of the algorithm.main ideaIt is to consider how the optimal local coefficient structure of a convolutional vision network approximates and covers easily accessible dense parts. This structure stacks commonly used convolutions (1x1, 3x3, 5x5) and pooling operations (3x3) together. It should be noted that the size of the convolution and pooling is the same, and the channels are increased. On the one hand, it increases the width of the network, and on the other hand, it increases the adaptability of the network at its most scale. The network convolutional layer can extract every detailed information of the input, and the 5x5 filter can also cover most of the input of the receiving layer. Pooling operations can also be performed to reduce space size and reduce overfitting. On top of these layers, a ReLu operation must be performed after each convolutional layer to increase nonlinear features.In order to reduce the calculation amount of the convolution kernel, a 1x1 convolution kernel needs to be added before 3x3 and 5x5 and after the maximum pooling. This leads to the revised Inception V1 The main purpose of 1x1 convolution is to reduce dimensionality and is also used to correct linear activation (ReLU). For example, the output of the previous layer is 100x100x128. After passing through a 5x5 convolution layer with 256 channels (stride=1, pad=2), the output data is 100x100x256, where the parameters of the convolution layer are 128x5x5x256= 819200. And if the output of the previous layer first passes through a 1x1 convolution layer with 32 channels, and then passes through a 5x5 convolution layer with 256 outputs, then the output data is still 100x100x256, but the amount of convolution parameters has been reduced to 128x1x1x32 + 32x5x5x256= 204800, which is reduced by approximately 4 times.

detailed explanation

(1) GoogLeNet adopts a modular structure (Inception structure) to facilitate addition and modification; (2) The network finally uses average pooling to replace the fully connected layer. This idea comes from NIN (Network in Network). Facts have proved that this can increase the accuracy by 0.6%. However, a fully connected layer is actually added at the end, mainly to facilitate flexible adjustment of the output; (3) Although full connection is removed, Dropout is still used in the network; (4) In order to avoid gradient disappearance, the network adds two additional auxiliary softmax to conduct the gradient forward (auxiliary classifier). The auxiliary classifier uses the output of an intermediate layer for classification and adds a smaller weight (0.3) to the final classification result. This is equivalent to model fusion. At the same time, it adds a back-propagated gradient signal to the network and provides additional regularization, which is very beneficial to the training of the entire network. In actual testing, these two additional softmax will be removed.
