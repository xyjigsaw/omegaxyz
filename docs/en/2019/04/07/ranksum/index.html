Introduction to Wilcoxon rank sum test and implementation in MATLAB

高级语言技术域Matlab机器学习Matlab
Wilcoxon rank-sum test (rank-sum test), sometimes also called Mann-Whitney U test, is another type of non-parametric test method. They do not make special assumptions about data distribution, so they can be applied to more complex data distribution situations.

applicability

(1) The overall distribution type of the data is unknown;
(2) The overall distribution type of the data is known, but it does not conform to the normal distribution;
(3) Some variables may not be accurately measured;
(4) Variances are uneven.

MATLAB

Two columns of data a, b         
[~,h1]=ranksum(a, b);
