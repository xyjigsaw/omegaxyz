Available models

  • Pollution concentration and dispersion models, such as the BW model of spatial analysis, analyze the development spatial situation of the transmission area.
  • The epidemic clustering and dispersing model uses the Moran'I coefficient to calculate the spatial correlation between epidemic areas and analyze whether the development of the epidemic shows spatial clustering or spread.
  • Factor influence model, SAR, MAR and other spatial structure regression models to identify influencing factors of spatial propagation
  • Cellular automaton + infectious disease spatial dynamic model
