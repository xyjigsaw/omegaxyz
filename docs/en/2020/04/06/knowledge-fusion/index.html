Knowledge Fusion (Entity Alignment) Notes

TechnologyMachine LearningKnowledge GraphsNatural Language Processing神经网络Natural Language Processing社交网络Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge fusion includes the following parts

Ontology matchingFocusing on discovering equivalent or similar classes, attributes or relationships at the pattern level, also known as ontology mapping and ontology alignmententity alignmentFocus on discovering different instances that refer to the same object in the real world, also known as entity resolution (resolution) and instance matching (instance matching)knowledge fusionGenerally, conflicts in the knowledge graph fusion process are resolved through technologies such as conflict detection and truth value discovery, and then the knowledge is associated and merged to finally form a consistent result.

Entity alignment

Currently, in terms of knowledge fusion, entity alignment based on embedding representation is a hot research topic. For more embedding-based entity alignment methods, please refer toAminerarticle. For example:1. MuGNN[1]Divided into two steps, ① complete the missing relationship to reconcile structural differences; ② combine the output of different channels through pooling technology.2. RDGCN[2]Relational information is processed through attentional interactions between KGs and their dual relational counterparts.3. AliNet[3]Since the neighborhood structure of the same entity is often non-isomorphic, neighborhood and pan-neighborhood information are comprehensively considered.4. OAG:LinKG[5]Aligning two networks with hundreds of millions of nodes, AMiner and Microsoft Academic, this research comprehensively utilizes LSTM, GNN, hashing and other technologies to efficiently process multiple types of nodes and different types of information, and achieves an applicable level of alignment (overall F1 value 96.81).

The challenge of knowledge integration

1. Embedding-based deep learning algorithm combined with knowledge reasoning (description logic, constraint rules) 2. Multi-modal knowledge graph fusion 3. Integration and update of large-scale knowledge graphs

Available tools

  • Open source implementation of cutting-edge algorithms for entity alignment:https://github.com/nju-websoft/OpenEA/
  • Ontology alignment—Falcon-AO, you need to input owl file:http://ws.nju.edu.cn/falcon-ao
  • Entity alignment—Dedupe, Python library, user-labeled minimal data, lightweight and fast training: pip install dedupe orhttps://github.com/datamade/dedupe
