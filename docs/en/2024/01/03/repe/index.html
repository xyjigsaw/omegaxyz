Highlight

  • Address:https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.01405
  • Repe is a way of improving the transparency of artificial intelligence systems by drawing on the insights of cognitive neuroscience.
  • In the field of AI interpretability, where earlier interpretability-related work was mainly centred on neurons, the article adopted some of the perspectives of cognitive neuroscience, emphasized the use of model information manifestations (essentially corresponding to the activation of each layer of the model) rather than neurons as the main subjects of research, proposed a set of basic methods for reading, understanding, modifying information forms and demonstrated the application of honesty, illusions, emotions, etc. in assessing and controlling LLM.
  • The method of reading the information forms presented in the article is Linear Artificial Tomography (LAT, linear artificial fault scan). Reading information forms is actually the structure of the high-dimensional space in the middle of the model. Ideally, much of this high-dimensional space is capable of giving corresponding concepts that humans can understand, but it is still far from that goal.

Methodology

Similar to the neuroimaging method, the LAT scan consists of three key steps:(1) Design stimulus and mission; (2) collection of neuroactivity; (3) construction of linear models.

1. Design stimulus and mission

Design the following templates for concepts: For example:
"Consider the amount of truthfulness in the following answer.\n\nQuestion: {q}\nAnswer: {a}\n\nThe amount of truthfulness in the answer is"
Why is it stopping? Based on the self-return training of the large-language model causal model model, is embedding actually contains information before the entire sentence (attention information, etc.); on the other hand, the model needs to predict a token behind the is, where the position token is typically a value or a degree of word.

2. Collection of neuroactivity

Generates input text by changing this part of the template. Each text is fed to the model to collect the activity of each layer of the model on last token (cause: the final output of the model is predicted on last token). Manipulate LLM

Using the RepE approach, the positive and negative examples are first designed, followed by a differential of [n_samples, lllm_dim] through the two variations, followed by a base vector [llm_dim] where the PCA method is used to achieve a downward direction. The base vector from which the lower dimension is obtained is integrated into the original model at the time of actual testing.

RepE's flaws

  1. Performance is highly correlated with the design of stimuli prompt, and finding a high quality prompt is key.
  2. While prompt takes the last position of the token to analyse the main ingredient for simple classification tasks, the evaluation of some indicators across the sentence is problematic.
    • For example, evaluation fluidity is OK, but there is a problem and a logical gap in assessing the logic/fact of the sentence. The generation of a conclusion requires consideration of continuity of reasoning, i.e. the intermediate step cannot be said.
